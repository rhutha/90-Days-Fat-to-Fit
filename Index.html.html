10 Rules for Movie-Star Body

1. Mind-Muscle

Feel every rep. Close eyes, squeeze, hold. Quality over quantity.

2. Never Skip Legs

Most testosterone release. Skip legs = slow upper body.

3. Protein Every 3h

5-6 small meals keep muscle synthesis elevated all day.

4. Progressive Overload

Add 1-2 reps or 1 set every week. No progress = no growth.

5. Sleep = Growth

95% repair in deep sleep. 7-8 hours min. No phone before bed.

6. Zero Alcohol/Junk

Alcohol kills testosterone 72 hrs. One beer undoes 3 days diet.

7. 10K Steps Daily

Burns 300-500 extra cal without eating into recovery.

8. Progress Photos

Same light, angle, time every 2 weeks. Mirror lies, photos don't.

9. Train to Failure

Last 2-3 reps where it burns are the ONLY ones that grow muscle.

10. Trust the Process

Wk 1-3: hard. Wk 4: signs. Wk 8: others notice. Wk 12: new you.
